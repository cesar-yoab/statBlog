Highest level of education helps predict your political inclination.

September 27, 2020 2-minute read

Introduction

Ever since Cambridge Analytica’s scandal more and more research has surfaced on how to predict political inclination, it seems that the era of big-data has completely changed how we view the world. In the search to find a “good” model for predicting political inclination, one variable that keeps comming up is that of education. The Pew Research Center has published numerous articles in which they discuss the relationship between highest education level and political inclination. Some of the more notorious ones, come from way back in 2015 and 2016. Surveys were performed in order to create these articles, which differs with our approach.

This time we are using already collected data from the 2019 Canadian Election Study (gcc)[http:://www.ces-eec.ca/]
